Welcome to Blossom Public School

Origin

Blossom came into being in July, 2001 as a nursery school committed to imparting education to little children aimed at laying a strong foundation focused on their future. The school is run by Wisdom foundation, an educational trust established for providing quality education to young boys and girls aspiring for excellence.

Mission

Blossom public school offers a right beginning for a bright future to young children both boys and girls. Education for excellence will be our enduring endeavour.
“Give us a bud and Take back a Flower” will be a never ending mission to all of us at Blossom.Caring for our children will be our continuous commitment and shaping their future will be our sincere purpose.

Objectives

The specific objectives of Blossom public school are:

  • To provide educational facilities aimed at laying a strong foundation for young children.
  • To improve individual skills like leadership and communications skills among young children
  • To impart value-based education and training to youth to make them socially responsible
  • To offer an environment to young children enabling them to experience the joy of learning through personalised instruction and guidance
  • To instil confidence and courage in young children and prepare them to face challenges without constraint.

Agenda

Blossom Public School has drafted for itself an educational agenda aimed at excellence. Starting from Pre K.G a child can begin schooling at Blossom and continue until completion of Standard XII. Keeping the educational career of every child in mind aimed at encouraging all round development, Blossom's core agenda will constitute the following

  • Emphasis on spoken English
  • Elimination of shyness
  • Extraction of talents
  • Enhancement of thinking power
  • Exercising body and mind
  • Exchanging of news and views
  • Encouraging extensive reading

Curriculum

The curriculum of Blossom Public School is being worked out keeping in mind the fact that too much of teaching too early will only burden them.

The focus is on the wholesome development of the child at his/her own pace. The KG follows the play-way method and activities form a major part of the day in this section.

Concentration on communication skills, creative thinking, character formation and career focused study will form the foundation on which the future of the child can be carefully built. Utmost care will be taken to continuously review the curriculum in consultation with other educators, professionals and parents in order to ensure that changes in curriculum will pave the way for establishing a progressive and practical system aimed at providing a purposeful educational career to the children at Blossom.
