Extra-Curricular Activities

JOY – JUST OPEN YOURSELF

JOY is an initiative by Blossom Public School to encourage our primary students to hone their skills through participation in various extra –curricular activities. JOY will be for students from Class III to Class V. These activities will be apart from the normal school curriculum. Students will be given ample opportunities to participate in these activities and exhibit their talents.Some of the activities conducted by JOY are given below

  • Quiz
  • Debates
  • Dumb Charades
  • Elocution
  • Spelling Bee
  • Essay Writing
  • Singing , Music & Dance

BLISS – BLOSSOM LITERARY & INTELLUCTUAL SOCIETY FOR STUDENTS

BLISS an initiative by Blossom Public School to encourage our primary students to hone their skills through participation in various extra –curricular activities. BLISS will be for students from Class VI to IX. These activities will be apart from the normal school curriculum.Some of the activities conducted by BLISS are given below

  • Elocution
  • Ad-zap
  • Talk shows
  • Quiz
  • Debates
  • Essay Writing
  • Singing & Dance

B-SURE

“B-Sure” is a Blossom initiative to create confidence in children. Starting from Std.VI, this course will be spread over five years laying a strong foundation for our children so that their confidence levels will be enhanced by the time they enter plus two level of their schooling. The curriculum for this course has been carefully thought out and precisely designed to provide our children exactly what is required to enable them lead a life filled with contentment and character.

The topics covered include Moral Values & Ethics, Attitude, Communication Skills, Self-esteem, Leadership, Anger Management, Motivation, Facing Failure, Team-Work, Public Speaking, Stress Management, Confidence Building, Goal Setting, Career Guidance, Physical Fitness, Real Happiness, Assertiveness, Time Management, Price of Freedom, Problem Solving & Will to Win.

Benefits of JOY & BLISS

  • Improving Communication Skills
  • Improving General Knowledge
  • Enhancing Creativity
  • Building Confidence
  • Eradicating Stage Fear
