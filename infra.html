INFRASTRUCTURE

AV ROOM

A fully equipped AV room with LCD projector and multimedia computer is available for use for AV- aided classes in the Nursery section.

SMART CLASSES

Audio visual education is being imparted to our students with the help of smart boards. We have partnered with Educomp for our Primary and Secondary sections. They provide the smart boards and the syllabus is in accordance with the CBSE curriculum.

COMPUTER LAB

Computer Education is being imparted in the form of a structured, student-friendly and innovative program called ‘Chrysalis’ in partnership with EZ Vidya. Two labs have been provided for use by the students of Primary and Secondary sections.



SCIENCE LAB

State of the Art laboratory facilities for Physics, Chemistry and Biology is available to aid in teaching concepts through practical lessons.

LANGUAGE LAB

Reading comprehensions are given to test the English language skills of the students. The lab has multiple headphones with a central sound system operated by the teacher and the students can read as well as hear the teacher read comprehensions , stories etc.

MATHS LAB

A lab with a smart board, computer and hands on lab material are used for maths related activities. There are various activities and exercises for all students from Class I to Class IX.



EDUSPORTS

Blossom Public School has partnered with Edusports (India’s leading physical education & school sports company) to provide a structured curriculum for physical education at the school from Std 1 upwards. The programme is designed to offer the same rigour in Sports and Physical Education (PE) as in academics. The curriculum and assessments have been designed in accordance with the CCE (Comprehensive and Continuous Evaluation) norms by CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education).

THREE D THEATRE

A Three D Theatre has been setup to explain concepts and theories with 3 dimensional effects. Educomp has setup this Three D Theatre and also provides content for all subjects including Science and Maths.

LIBRARY

There is a common library for the Primary and Secondary sections. We have a wide range of books covering various subjects. Currently there are 6000 books available in our library and we subscribe to leading newspapers and magazines



MOUNTAIN TOP – MULTI PURPOSE HALL

A fully equipped air conditioned multipurpose hall with a LCD projector and sound system is available for extracurricular activities, events, competitions & other functions.

KIDZ KITCHEN

Lunch is provided for children at the primary and secondary sections on an optional basis. Nutritional and healthy food is prepared and served at the Kidz Kitchen.

INFIRMARY

An Infirmary with a small dispensary and six beds is open for our children who fall sick during school hours.



TRANSPORT

The School operates Vans/Buses for the daily transport of our children. School Transport is available for children from L.K.G and upwards.
